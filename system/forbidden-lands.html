What is this system

Open-world hexploration (exploring the map hex by hex, like fog of war) in dark fantasy. For 300 years the Blood Mist made travel deadly — people lived in isolated settlements. Now the mist has cleared, and you are the first to dare explore the forgotten lands. Build a stronghold, uncover the map, survive.

Setting

A grim world evoking The Witcher and Dark Souls. Ruined castles, forgotten demons, clan conflicts. Every hex holds a secret: an abandoned village, a demon's lair, or ruins of a civilization that perished during the Blood Mist.

What it looks like at the table
Camping in the ruins. Random event roll — 'Strangers at the camp.' Food is running out, one day of water left. The swordsman pushes an Endurance roll — success, but his sword's gear bonus drops by 1. The strangers offer a trade: provisions for your map. Without the map you'll get lost. Without food — you won't reach the stronghold.

Key mechanics

Stronghold building

Find ruins — build your refuge. Every structure grants bonuses and shifts the region's geopolitics.

Hexploration

An open map full of secrets. Each hex is a random or planned location. The world reveals itself with every step.

Camp and resources

Food, water, sleep — all tracked. Making camp in dangerous lands is a survival mini-game.

Critical wounds

Wounds maim permanently. A lost eye won't grow back. Combat is the last resort, not the first.
