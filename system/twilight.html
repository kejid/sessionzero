Twilight: 2000

"World War III is over. You lost. Now — survive."

DiceПул d6–d12

Foundry VTTOfficial
Complexity
What is this system

A military sandbox about surviving after nuclear war. You are the remnants of a shattered NATO unit in Poland (or Sweden). Command is silent, fuel is running out, ammo is scarce, and winter is coming. Can you make it home — or at least survive until spring?

Setting

An alternate year 2000. World War III happened. Nuclear strikes destroyed major cities. Europe is a zone of chaos: marauders, deserters, local warlords. Polish winter, ruined infrastructure, and the remnants of civilization.

What it looks like at the table
You have 12 rounds for the M16, half a tank of diesel in the APC, and two wounded. The village ahead is controlled by a local warlord. He offers: a tank of diesel for a job — clear the marauders on the bridge. Persuasion roll on d10+d8 — failure. He raises the price: diesel AND your medkit. January. Three months until spring.
Playstyle
Survival Tactical Sandbox Social Modern Post-Apoc
Key mechanics

Realistic combat

Ballistics, cover, suppression. A single bullet can kill. Combat is desperate, short, and terrifying.

Scarcity economy

Fuel, ammo, medicine — worth their weight in gold. Every shot is a decision: is the target worth a round?

Vehicles

Tanks, APCs, trucks — your home on wheels. Repair and modification are a separate layer of gameplay.

Hexploration

An open map of Poland with points of interest, factions, and random events. Where to go is your call.
